VehicutaMax is a comprehensive vehicle management application designed to help users track, maintain, and optimize their vehicle usage. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the VehicutaMax application and explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

To provide, maintain, and improve VehicutaMax features and functionality, including vehicle tracking, maintenance scheduling, and cost analysis.

Personalization

To personalize your experience by providing customized recommendations, maintenance reminders, and vehicle insights based on your specific vehicles and usage patterns.

To communicate with you about app updates, new features, maintenance reminders, security alerts, and support responses.

To analyze usage patterns, understand user preferences, and improve the app's performance, features, and user experience.

To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure, or access. These measures include:
